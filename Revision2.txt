  *local variable
  *static variable
  *non-static variable
  *Parameterized variable
  
  local-These variables are declared and initialized in a method , their 
         scope lies between the method. we cannot access in other method.
  static -These variables are declared with a static keyword and they are declared and 
          initialized at the level of class and we can access in any classes
  Non-static-These variables are declared  and initialized at the level of class 
           without keyword static and they are associated to the objects.
  Parameter-These variables are declared inside the method paranthesise.
